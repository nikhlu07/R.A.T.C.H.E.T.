Core Technology

R.A.T.C.H.E.T. is built upon a multi-layered architecture that combines cutting-edge technologies in decentralized identity, quantum security, and artificial intelligence. The foundation of this architecture is the Key Event Receipt Infrastructure (KERI), providing a secure and verifiable identity layer.

KERIA: Identity & Trust Layer

The foundation of R.A.T.C.H.E.T. is built on KERIA, providing a decentralized, self-certifying identity framework. This ensures that every component, physical or digital, has a portable, secure, and unambiguous identity, forming the root of trust for the entire system.

Quantum: Security Layer

This layer leverages principles of quantum mechanics to provide unparalleled security. This includes quantum-entangled identifiers and post-Shor encryption simulations, creating a theoretically unbreakable link between identities and communications.

Neural: Intelligence Layer

A simulated neural mesh that enables collective intelligence and autonomous learning. The system can detect anomalies, predict failures, and adapt its understanding of the physical world in real-time, mimicking a form of industrial consciousness.

Reality: Digital Twin Layer

This layer maintains a high-fidelity, real-time digital twin of physical assets. It uses a consensus mechanism to ensure the digital representation is an accurate reflection of reality, and allows for 'temporal debugging'—reviewing past states to understand events.
